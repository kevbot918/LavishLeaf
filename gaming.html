Welcome to LavishLeaf Gaming

LavishLeaf Gaming is our newest division — a full-spectrum gaming studio growing from the ground up right alongside our farms, compost, and sports divisions. We believe games can do more than entertain; they can educate, connect communities, and create real-world impact.

Our mission is to develop high-quality game systems and assets for other developers, create our own original titles, host servers for popular games, and grow a following through YouTube and social media content — all while staying true to Lavish Leaf's core values of community, education, and meaningful living.

Why Educational Gaming?

Games are one of the most powerful learning tools ever created. When your game teaches players how to manage resources, plan sustainable systems, work as a team, or solve real-world problems — that's education in action. You don't need a 501(c)(3) to build games with purpose.

LavishLeaf Gaming will weave real educational value into our titles — from farming and sustainability mechanics that mirror our real-world operations, to sports strategy games that teach teamwork and decision-making. Schools, parents, and players looking for more than just entertainment will find it here.

Educational content also opens doors: grants, school district licensing, partnerships with libraries and community programs, and a loyal audience that values what you're building. It aligns perfectly with everything Lavish Leaf already stands for.

What We Do

Unity Asset Store

We design and publish professional-grade game systems and assets on the Unity Asset Store. Our tools are built for developers who want polished, reusable systems — vehicle controllers, game mechanics, AI behaviors, and more. Every asset is documented and designed with the developer experience in mind.

Game Development

LavishLeaf Gaming is developing original video game titles that blend entertainment with educational purpose. Our games draw inspiration from real-world topics — sustainable farming, community management, sports strategy — to create experiences that are fun to play and genuinely meaningful.

Game Server Hosting

We host dedicated game servers for popular multiplayer titles, providing reliable, community-focused server infrastructure. Whether for organized leagues, private communities, or public play, our servers are maintained with performance and uptime in mind.

Content & Marketing

LavishLeaf Gaming produces dev logs, tutorials, gameplay content, and promotional material on YouTube and across social platforms. We document the journey of building a studio from the ground up — sharing knowledge, building community, and marketing our games and assets along the way.

Current Projects

LavishLeaf Gaming is currently in active development. Our first Unity Asset Store releases are focused on vehicle and physics game systems — the same high-quality systems we use in our own games, made available for the developer community.

Game titles and server hosting services are in early planning stages. Follow us on social media and YouTube for development updates, behind-the-scenes content, and early access opportunities.
