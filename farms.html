What We Grow

Our farm produces a variety of organic vegetables, fruits, and plants grown using sustainable, chemical-free methods. From Brussels sprouts and zucchini to corn, squash, sunflowers, and green beans — we grow what communities need.

We also cultivate seedlings and transplants for home gardeners and community gardens. All produce is grown right here in Eastern Oklahoma, tended with care and love for the land.
