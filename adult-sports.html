Adult 7v7 Soccer Summer League

Fall League is starting now and will run until Christmas. Games will be played at the Lake Eufaula Eastside Ballpark in Eufaula, OK on Sundays 3pm to 8pm depending on when teams are available. Teams may be added in throughout the season and playoffs will be adjusted accordingly.

We are an organization so all registration fees will go towards insurance, field maintenance, equipment, league management, medals, trophies, and prizes!
