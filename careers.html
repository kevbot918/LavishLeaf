Lavish Leaf Careers

At Lavish Leaf we know that our greatest assets are our people. We take pride in providing our employees and customers quality products and services to enhance the quality of life and improve the work-life balance.

Our current positions are volunteer opportunities, however, our aim is to offer full-time and part-time positions with competitive wages and exceptional employee benefits.

Current Volunteer Positions

Board Member

Description: Join Lavish Leaf and help promote and develop community centered, sustainable lifestyles.

Responsibilities may include

  • Attend monthly or quarterly board meetings
  • Create, develop, and promote Lavish Leaf products, services, and events
  • Assist in obtaining sponsors, fundraising, and grants
  • Interview, hire, schedule, and coordinate staff for events and services
  • Educate others and ensure Lavish Leaf remains true to its mission and vision

Only volunteer opportunities available!

Farm Hand

Description: Assist with day to day operations of the farm. Watering, planting, harvesting, and more.

Responsibilities may include

  • Water plants when needed and check and monitor soil and plants
  • Harvest produce and wash and package produce
  • Fill germination trays, make soil blocks, and sow seeds
  • Transplant seedlings, repot plants, and clean equipment
  • De-weed garden beds and monitor garden for diseases and pests
  • Mix and make compost teas and fertilizer and spread onto garden beds

Only volunteer opportunities available!

Social Media Coordinator

Description: Promote Lavish Leaf and interact with our customers on Lavish Leaf's social media accounts.

Responsibilities may include

  • Create content, posts, and ads to promote our products and services
  • Reply and respond to comments and direct messages
  • Support, guide, interact, and connect our customers through social media
  • Collaborate and assist management with promotional goods and services
  • Regularly take pictures and videos of the farm and processes

Only volunteer opportunities available!

Delivery Driver

Description: Pick-up compost and recyclables from customers. Deliver produce and plants.

Responsibilities may include

  • Using your own personal truck to pick-up compost and deliver to Lavish Leaf Farms
  • Using your own personal truck to pick-up recycling and deliver to recycling centers
  • May be required to sort recycling materials
  • Teach and inform customers on accepted composting and recycling materials
  • Collaborate with and train other pick-up drivers

Only volunteer opportunities available!
