Compost Pick-Up Service

Compost Pick-up

$20.00 every month

Reduce your trash and start saving the environment with us! Did you know that when food scraps and other compostable items go to landfills the methane gas released instead of CO2 is 80x worse for the environment!

COMPOST PICK-UP SERVICE: We will give you and switch out a 5 gallon bucket every 1–2 weeks depending on how quickly you fill it up. We recommend using your own indoor compost bin and regularly dumping it into our 5 gallon bucket.

Items that you can compost instead of allowing it to go into a landfill: food scraps, coffee grounds, lawn clippings, leaves, mulch, ink-free paper, and many other compostable items.

* Pick-up days are your choice! Choose whichever day of the week you prefer!
